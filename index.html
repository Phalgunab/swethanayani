The Artist

A Life Lived in Dance

Swetha Nayani began her journey into Bharatanatyam at the age of five, trained rigorously in the Pandanallur style — celebrated for its geometric precision and deep devotional spirit.

Her abhinaya carries the weight of sacred texts and the lightness of lotus petals. She has performed across the grandest stages of India and abroad, weaving the stories of Shiva, Vishnu, and the cosmic feminine into living poetry of movement.

The Guru

A Living Legend

Pramod Kumar Reddy, a versatile Bharatanatyam dancer, teacher, and Founder-President of Abhinetri Arts Academy!

He is a passionate Bharatanatyam dancer, teacher, and cultural advocate dedicated to preserving and promoting India's rich artistic heritage.

As an educator and founder of a thriving dance institution, Pramod continues to create opportunities for aspiring artists, fostering an environment where tradition meets excellence and passion transforms into performance.

Abhinetri Arts Academy

Pass the Flame Forward

Founded to transmit authentic Bharatanatyam to the next generation, Abhinetri Arts Academy follows the Pandanallur curriculum — with equal emphasis on nritta, nritya, and natya.

With students from age 5 to 55, the academy has produced performers who have graced stages across India, the UK, the US, and Southeast Asia.
